<Section icon={Trophy} title="How to Earn Points Fast for Shop Unlocks">

Unlocking the best cosmetic items requires a steady stream of in-game currency. Super Battle Golf utilizes a points-based scoring system that rewards both speed and skill. Unlike traditional golf, simply taking fewer shots is not the only path to victory. You must balance your stroke count with aggressive play, map navigation, and speed running to the hole.

To build up your currency reserves quickly, you need to understand how points are distributed at the end of each hole. Finishing first grants a massive speed bonus, while scoring under par provides substantial technical points. Conversely, getting caught in hazards or receiving penalty strokes will severely limit your round payouts.

  <Step num="1" title="Master the 100% Power Boost">

  <Step num="2" title="Utilize Map Shortcuts and Checkpoints">

  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Secure Knockouts with Weapons">

  <Step num="4" title="Avoid Water Hazards and Resets">

  </Step>
