<Section icon={Target} title="How to Unlock Cosmetics in Super Battle Golf">

Unlocking new vanity items requires a mix of match participation, currency management, and challenge completion. While some items are available to everyone by default, the most prestigious cosmetics require you to prove your skills on the green. By playing in both public and private lobbies, you will naturally accumulate the resources and milestones needed to expand your wardrobe.

Players can purchase items directly from the in-game shop using coins earned by completing holes, finishing matches, and executing stylish plays. Alternatively, special limited-time events and seasonal updates offer direct progression tracks where cosmetics are awarded immediately upon reaching specific milestones.

<Callout type="tip" title="Prioritize Your Coins">
Save your hard-earned coins for high-tier score effects and ball trails first. Because the area around the cup is often crowded with up to eight players throwing smoke bombs and orbital strikes, these visual effects are the most noticeable way to celebrate a successful putt.
</Callout>

If you are ready to start customizing your character, follow these steps to secure and equip your very first custom outfit.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Earn In-Game Currency">
    Complete multiplayer matches in either public servers or private friend lobbies. Finishing holes quickly and placing high on the leaderboard awards the maximum amount of coins per match.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Browse the Customization Shop">
    Navigate to the main menu and open the Customization Shop. Here, you can preview locked items, check their coin prices, and see if they require specific gameplay achievements to purchase.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Complete Target Achievements">
    Identify achievements that reward exclusive cosmetics. Focus on straightforward tasks first, such as finishing your first 18 holes or accumulating a set number of player knockouts.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Equip Your New Gear">
    Go to the Character Customization screen from the lobby menu. Select your desired slot, choose your newly unlocked item, and apply the changes before queuing for your next match.
  </Step>
