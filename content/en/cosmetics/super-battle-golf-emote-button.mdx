<Steps>

  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Navigate to Controls">
    Select the **Controls** tab. Here you will see a list of all current key bindings for both keyboard and detected controllers.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Remap the Emote Key">
    Click on the current binding for 'Emote/Dance' and press the new key or button you wish to assign.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Apply and Test">
    Save your changes and enter a practice round to ensure the new **super battle golf emote button** configuration works as expected.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Steam Input Tip">
For PlayStation and Switch users, enabling **Steam Input** in the game's library properties is recommended. This allows the game to recognize these controllers as XInput devices, though in-game prompts will still show Xbox labels (A/B/X/Y).
</Callout>
