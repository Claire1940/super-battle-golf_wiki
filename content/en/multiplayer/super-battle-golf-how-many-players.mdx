<Section icon={Swords} title="Multiplayer Modes and Sabotage Mechanics">

Super Battle Golf blends the core mechanics of golf with the frantic, item-based combat of kart racers. Because everyone plays at the same time, you do not have to wait for your opponents to complete their turns. You swing, chase after your ball, and use various items to sabotage anyone who gets ahead of you.

The physics engine is arcade-focused. There is no wind resistance, meaning your shots will travel straight unless you overcharge your swing. Overcharging introduces a heavy hook or slice, but it can also be used strategically to lock onto and beam opponents in the head with your ball, granting you a temporary speed boost.

<Callout type="warning" title="Watch the Choke Points">

<Section icon={Target} title="How to Host and Join Matches">

Super Battle Golf utilizes a peer-to-peer hosting system, meaning the player who creates the lobby acts as the host. Despite this decentralized setup, connection performance remains smooth, allowing players to drop in and drop out without causing game-wide lag. If you cannot gather eight friends to fill a lobby, you can easily open your private session to the public.
