<Section icon={Target} title="Coordinated Combat and Item Usage">

Items are the backbone of Super Battle Golf. In team scenarios, using them correctly can turn a losing match into a comeback. You should categorize your inventory based on your team's current position on the course.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Assessment">
    Identify if your team is leading or trailing. If leading, prioritize defensive items; if trailing, use mobility or offensive tools.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Distribution">
    Share item roles. A "Striker" should hold Railguns or Orbital Lasers, while a "Defender" keeps Smoke Bombs and Flash Cameras ready.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Execution">
    Synchronize your attacks. Using a Thunderstorm to disrupt enemies while a teammate takes a precise shot is a guaranteed way to secure a hole.
  </Step>
</Steps>

| Item Class | Best For | Effectiveness |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Offensive** | Railgun, Orbital Laser | Excellent |
| **Defensive** | Smoke Bomb, Jumbo Burger | Very Good |
| **Disruption** | Flash Camera, Thunderstorm | Good |
| **Mobility** | Launch items, Cart usage | Situational |

<Callout type="warning" title="Team Coordination Warning">
Avoid using area-of-effect items like the Thunderstorm if your teammates are in the blast radius. Communication is vital to prevent friendly fire disruptions.
</Callout>
