<Section icon={Swords} title="Multiplayer and Cross-Play Mechanics">

Multiplayer is the core focus of the game. Up to 16 players can join a single match, turning a traditional round of golf into a chaotic battleground. Because the game features cross-platform multiplayer, console players can compete directly against PC players. This integration ensures a healthy matchmaking pool, though it requires console users to understand the subscription requirements associated with online play.

To participate in online lobbies, console players must have an active subscription to Xbox Game Pass (Essential, Premium, or Ultimate). Without this, gameplay is limited to single-player modes. The cross-play feature can be toggled in the settings menu for players who prefer to match only with users on the same platform.
