<Section icon={Swords} title="Gameplay Mechanics and Item Sandbox">

The core gameplay loop is a brilliant fusion of arcade golf physics and kart-racer combat. Players do not take turns; instead, everyone swings simultaneously in a mad dash to sink their ball. The physics engine is deliberately arcade-like, though it requires precise timing. Overcharging a shot introduces a severe slice, curving the ball wildly offline unless the player is currently in a losing position, which activates a helpful homing lock-on mechanic.

Items collected from Mario Kart-style mystery boxes add a layer of tactical disruption. From standard mobility boosts like coffee and jump boots to devastating offensive tools like rocket launchers and orbital lasers, players must constantly balance speed-running the course with active combat.
