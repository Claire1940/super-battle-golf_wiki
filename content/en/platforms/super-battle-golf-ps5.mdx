<Section icon={Shield} title="Biome Guide: Navigating the 27 Holes">

The game features three distinct biomes, each presenting unique environmental challenges. Mastery of **super battle golf ps5** requires learning the "lines" for each hole—the most efficient path from tee to green while avoiding hazards.

| Biome | Hazard Type | Difficulty | Key Strategy |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Forest** | **Water/Trees** | Easy | Focus on straight drives and basic items |
| **Desert** | **Sand/Canyons** | Medium | Use high-arc shots to clear dunes |
| **Magma/Tech** | **Lava/Moving Platforms** | Hard | Save jump boots for platforming sections |

Each round shuffles the holes within a biome, keeping the experience fresh. The "Tech" biomes often feature verticality, where using the mouse wheel to adjust your shot angle to 45° or higher is mandatory to reach elevated greens.

<Callout type="info" title="Out of Bounds Rules">
If your ball or character falls out of bounds, you respawn at the last "checkpoint" you passed. Always aim for the center of the fairway to unlock these respawn points, as skipping them to take a shortcut is high-risk, high-reward.
</Callout>
