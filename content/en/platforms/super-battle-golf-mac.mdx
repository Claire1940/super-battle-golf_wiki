<Section icon={Target} title="Core Gameplay Mechanics & Controls">

To succeed in **super battle golf mac**, you must master the delicate balance between rapid locomotion and precise golf shots. Unlike traditional golf games where players take turns, this game is a real-time race to the hole. The controls are designed to be fast, responsive, and highly physical.

The power meter moves exceptionally fast, making precise timing a major skill gap. Players can use hotkeys to quickly adjust their shot angle, allowing them to shift from a long-distance drive to a short putt in milliseconds. Additionally, physical combat is always an option; winding up a club swing near another player allows you to smack them directly, knocking them down and granting you a substantial movement speed boost.

| Action | Keybind | Tactical Use |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Aim Shot** | Mouse Movement | Line up your trajectory along the green. |
| **Adjust Angle** | Scroll Wheel / Keys 1-4 | Shift between 0° (putting) and 45° (high loft). |
| **Charge Shot** | Hold Right-Click | Power up your shot; overcharging causes a slice. |
| **Melee Strike** | Left-Click (Near Player) | Attack rivals to disable them and gain a speed boost. |
| **Sprint** | Shift Key | Fast movement between shots; consumes stamina. |
