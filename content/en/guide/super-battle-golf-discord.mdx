<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Beginner Progression Guide">

Entering a match with 7 other players using orbital lasers can be overwhelming. Follow this structured approach to transition from a novice to a consistent winner on the fairways.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Master the Swing Gauge">
    Learn to judge distance without relying solely on maximum power. Partial power shots are often safer when navigating narrow platforms or approaching the green.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Prioritize Checkpoints">
    Always touch the nearest checkpoint before attempting a risky shortcut. If you are knocked out by an item or fall out of bounds, you will respawn at the last activated gate.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Inventory Awareness">
    You have limited item slots. Do not hoard low-tier items; use them to disrupt nearby opponents and clear space for high-impact pickups like the **Smoke Bomb**.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Movement and Recovery">
    Start running toward your ball's landing zone the moment it leaves the club. Use the jump-dive combination to clear small hazards and reach your ball faster than your rivals.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="The Final Approach">
    The area around the cup is a "kill zone." Use defensive items or wait for the initial wave of attacks to dissipate before attempting your final putt.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Winning Mentality">
In Super Battle Golf, finishing second with a clean scorecard is often better than finishing first with maximum strokes. Focus on your own game and only attack others when they directly block your path.
</Callout>
