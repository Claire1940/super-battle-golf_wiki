<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Swing Techniques and Homing Shots">

Adjusting your swing style based on the terrain is crucial for maintaining control of the ball. The game features two primary shot types: putts and chips. Putts keep the ball low to the ground, making them ideal for flat surfaces, while chips loft the ball into the air to bypass obstacles or rough terrain.

When executing a putt, aim slightly beyond your target distance. The friction of the green typically slows the ball down faster than expected. Conversely, when chipping, aim slightly short of your target. Chipped shots carry significant forward momentum upon landing and will roll past your intended destination if you swing with full power.

| Shot Type | Target Adjustment | Ideal Terrain | Roll Behavior |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Putt | Aim over target | Flat greens, short distance | Low roll, high friction |
| Chip | Aim under target | Rough, sand, obstacles | High bounce, long roll |
| Homing | Automatic lock-on | Distant targets, hazards | Direct path, high velocity |

Maxing out your energy bar unlocks the homing shot mechanic. This special ability allows your ball to lock onto targets automatically, ensuring a direct path to the hole or specific objects on the field. You can even target active mines to detonate them from a safe distance.

<Callout type="tip" title="Homing Shot Setup">
You can trigger a homing shot on a mine by dropping it directly in front of you. However, doing so consumes your entire item stack. Alternatively, throw the mine forward and hit it before the arming timer expires to save resources.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Swords} title="Combat Tactics and Map Control">

Combat is a fundamental element of the gameplay loop. Striking other players does not just slow them down; it actively increases your own movement speed. Engaging in combat is highly recommended when traversing long fairways.

When confronting an opponent, use a quick, uncharged club strike to knock them off balance. Once they are downed, charge a maximum power swing to launch them off the map or into nearby water hazards. This tactic temporarily removes them from play, giving you a clear path to the green.
