<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Disarming Guide">

Disarming a landmine requires precise positioning and timing. If you approach the hazard too quickly or without the proper equipment active, you risk detonating the explosive, which launches your character away and ruins the attempt. Follow these steps to complete the process safely.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Obtain the Landmine Item">
    Acquire a landmine during gameplay. In standard matches, these items spawn inside item boxes scattered across the course. Alternatively, have a friendly player acquire one.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Deploy the Hazard">
    Have the player holding the landmine drop it onto a flat section of the course. The deploying player should right-click to throw the mine forward onto the ground.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Approach with Club Equipped">
    Equip your golf club and walk slowly toward the deployed landmine. Keeping your club active is necessary for the interaction prompt to appear.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Trigger the Disarm Action">
    Once you are within close range of the mine, press the interaction key (default key: **E**). Your character will perform the disarm animation, securing the achievement.
  </Step>
</Steps>

| Action Type | Control Input | Stance Required | Resulting Effect |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Deploy Mine** | Right-Click | Item Active | Places explosive hazard on the ground |
| **Approach Mine** | Movement Keys | Club Equipped | Triggers the interaction prompt |
| **Disarm Mine** | E Key | Club Equipped | Safely removes mine, awards achievement |

<Callout type="tip" title="Stance Check">
Ensure your character is not sprinting when approaching the explosive. Running too fast can cause you to overshoot the interaction zone and trigger the detonation radius.
</Callout>
