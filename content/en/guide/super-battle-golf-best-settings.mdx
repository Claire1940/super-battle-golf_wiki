<Section icon={Target} title="Gameplay and Swing Sensitivity Settings">

The core of the game is the swing mechanic. Finding the **super battle golf best settings** for sensitivity can be the difference between a Hole-in-One and falling off a cliff. Sensitivity affects how fast your aiming reticle moves and how quickly you can adjust your camera during the chaotic movement phase between shots.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Calibrate Aiming Sensitivity">
    Start with a medium sensitivity. If you find yourself over-correcting during putts, lower the horizontal sensitivity. You need enough speed to track a moving player but enough precision to hit a narrow green.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Adjust Camera Follow Speed">
    In the settings, look for "Camera Follow." Setting this to high ensures that after you hit the ball, the camera snaps to your character quickly, allowing you to start running toward the next checkpoint immediately.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Fine-tune Swing Power HUD">
    Ensure the power meter is set to "High Visibility" mode. In maps like the Frozen Fairway, the bright snow can sometimes wash out the default UI colors.
  </Step>
</Steps>

| Sensitivity Tier | Value (0-100) | Best For |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Low** | 15-30 | Precision putting and long-range sniping. |
| **Balanced** | 45-60 | General play, recommended for beginners. |
| **High** | 75-90 | Aggressive combat and rapid navigation. |

<Callout type="success" title="Accuracy Boost">
Lowering your "Vertical Sensitivity" specifically can help keep your aim level with the horizon, making it easier to line up straight drives on flat fairways without accidentally aiming into the ground or sky.
</Callout>
