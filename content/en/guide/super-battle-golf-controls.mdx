<Section icon={Target} title="Advanced Shot Types: Putting and Chipping">

Precision is the secondary pillar of **super battle golf controls**. While the chaos of combat happens around you, you still need to get the ball into the hole in as few swings as possible to maximize your score. The game differentiates between "Putting" and "Chipping" through physical positioning and power levels.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Select Your Shot Type">
    Determine if you need a "Putt Over" or a "Chip Under." Putting is for flat surfaces and short distances, while chipping helps you clear small obstacles or rough terrain.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Align the Trajectory">
    Use your camera to line up the shot. Remember that the ball can bounce off environmental objects and even other players.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Charge Energy">

<Section icon={Shield} title="Mine Management and Hazard Disarming">

Mines are the most common hazard you will encounter, often deployed by other players or spawned by the map itself. Walking into an armed mine will send you flying, likely costing you the round. Learning the specific **super battle golf controls** for disarming is vital for survival.

**How to Safely Handle Mines:**
1. **Identify the State**: An armed mine will have a visible glow or timer.
2. **Approach with Caution**: Do not run toward an armed mine.
3. **Slow Walk**: Hold the **Alt key** (or designated slow-walk button) to approach without triggering the sensor.
4. **Disarm**: Press **E** once you are within range to pick the mine up and add it to your inventory.

| Mine Action | Requirement | Outcome |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Throw Mine** | Item Slot + Click | Launches mine forward; starts a short arming timer |
| **Drop Mine** | Inventory Action | Drops the entire stack at your feet; arms instantly |
| **Disarm Mine** | Alt + E | Safely removes the hazard and gives you an item |
| **Homing Mine** | Max Energy Swing | Turns a stationary mine into a seeking projectile |

<Callout type="success" title="Inventory Tip">
If you finish a round while standing on an item box, your character will continue to pick up items until their inventory is full, even while you can't move. Use this to stock up for the next hole!
</Callout>
