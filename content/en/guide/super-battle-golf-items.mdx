The **Rocket Launcher** is particularly feared because it removes the need for precise aiming. Once fired, the target must either have a shield active or find immediate physical cover, which is often impossible on open fairways. Meanwhile, the **Electromagnet** has become a staple for players who have already reached the green. Activating it just as you prepare your final putt ensures that no one can interrupt your winning stroke.

<Section icon={Target} title="A-Tier and B-Tier: Situational Excellence">

Not every item needs to be a game-breaker to be useful. A-Tier items are consistently strong but may require specific conditions to reach their full potential. B-Tier items, on the other hand, are often high-risk/high-reward or simply outclassed by S-Tier alternatives.

The **Air Horn** is the standout of the A-Tier. It doesn't physically move an opponent, but the loud noise and screen shake can cause a player to mistime their shot power, leading to disastrous overshooting. The **Golf Cart** is another strong contender, offering team-based mobility and the ability to physically ram into rivals, though it is a large target and easily avoided by experienced players.

<Callout type="success" title="Strategy Tip">
