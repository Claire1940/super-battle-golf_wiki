<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step: Taking the Perfect Shot">

Mastering the mechanics of the swing is vital for maintaining a low score. Follow these steps to ensure your **super battle golf balls** always find the fairway.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Survey the Terrain">
    Check for water hazards, trees, and landmines. If the path is clear, aim for the fairway. If there are obstacles, prepare to use a higher loft angle.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Adjust Your Angle">
    Use the scroll wheel to set your trajectory. For long distances, 45 degrees is standard. For chipping onto the green, use a lower angle (10-20 degrees) to prevent the ball from bouncing past the hole.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Charge and Release">
    Hold Left Click to fill the power meter. Watch the meter closely; over-charging is the leading cause of penalty strokes in competitive play.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Follow Up with Traversal">
    Immediately after the ball lands, move toward it. If you have a Golf Cart or Spring Boots, use them now to close the gap before your opponents can recover.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="Penalty Strokes">
Going out of bounds or into deep water results in a penalty stroke. Your ball will be reset to its previous position, and you will lose valuable time on the round timer.
</Callout>
