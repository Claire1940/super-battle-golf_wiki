<Section icon={Compass} title="Utility and Mobility: Staying Ahead">

In Super Battle Golf, speed is often more valuable than raw firepower. If you can reach your ball and sink it before your opponents even arrive, their weapons become irrelevant. This is where utility items shine, providing the speed and disruption necessary to maintain lead positioning.

**Coffee vs. Golf Cart**
The debate between Coffee and the Golf Cart often comes down to the specific map. Coffee provides a 10-second personal speed boost that feels smooth and responsive. It is ideal for long fairways where you need a quick burst to outpace a nearby rival. The Golf Cart, however, is a chaotic powerhouse. It can carry up to eight players, meaning you can hijack the mobility of your entire squad or use it to ram into opponents, knocking them off course.

| Utility Item | Duration | Capacity | Main Advantage |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Coffee** | 10 Seconds | 1 Player | Precise speed control |
| **Golf Cart** | Until Destroyed | 8 Players | Massive disruption and ramming |
| **Air Horn** | Instant | N/A | Psychological sabotage |

The Air Horn is a unique utility tool often referred to as the "Friendship Destroyer." It doesn't physically move your opponent; instead, it targets their focus. By blasting the horn while an opponent is charging their shot, you can cause them to overhit or miss their timing entirely.

<Callout type="success" title="Golf Cart Pro Tip">
The Golf Cart is most effective when used to run over opponents who are currently lining up a shot. The disruption to their camera and positioning is often more frustrating than a standard stun.
</Callout>

Not all **super battle golf weapons** are easy to use. The B-Tier consists of items that are devastating in the right hands but can easily backfire or be avoided by experienced players. Mastering these tools requires a deep understanding of player psychology and map geometry.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="The Landmine Trap">
    Instead of placing landmines in the open, drop them behind corners or immediately next to an opponent's ball. This forces them to either take the stun or waste time navigating around the obstacle.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="The Orbital Laser Strike">
    The laser is stationary. Use it on the green or near the hole where opponents are forced to congregate. Calling it down on a moving target is a waste of a rare resource.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Managing Elephant Gun Recoil">
    The Elephant Gun provides two massive stun shots, but the knockback will push you backward. Ensure there isn't a hazard or a cliff behind you before firing, or you might stun yourself more than the enemy.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Combat Synergies
Combining items can lead to unstoppable plays. For example, using **Coffee** to reach a high-vantage point before unleashing an **Orbital Laser** on the crowded green below can clear the path for your final putt. Similarly, using **Spring Boots** to close the distance before firing a **Dueling Pistol** ensures your shot connects, as the weapon is much more reliable at close range.

| Strategy Type | Recommended Items | Primary Goal |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |

| **Speedster** | Spring Boots / Coffee | Win by pure velocity |
| **Tactical** | Electromagnet / Landmine | Protect lead and set traps |
