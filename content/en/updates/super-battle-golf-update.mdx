<Section icon={Map} title="New City Maps & Shortcut Strategies">

The 18 new City maps introduced in the **super battle golf update** emphasize verticality and timing. Unlike the relatively flat fairways of the Forest or Coast biomes, City holes feature multiple layers of elevation, moving vehicular hazards, and devastating out-of-bounds drops.

To secure fast times, you must master the art of the bank shot, utilizing angled skyscraper walls to redirect your ball around sharp corners. Additionally, knowing when to bypass a checkpoint to execute a risky shortcut can mean the difference between first and last place.

| Map Section | Key Hazards | Recommended Route | Shortcut Opportunity |
|---|---|---|---|
| Skyscraper Rooftops | Strong winds, narrow ledge drops | Use controlled, medium-power shots to stay on flat platforms. | Skip intermediate platforms by aiming directly for lower rooftops. |
| Highway Overpass | Moving vehicle traffic, barriers | Time your swing to release immediately after a vehicle pass. | Use a high-angle shot to clear the highway barriers entirely. |
| Underpass Tunnels | Tight spaces, player congestion | Wait for explosive hazards to clear before rushing through. | Bounce bank shots off the curved walls to bypass choke points. |
| Downtown Green | Area attacks, close-range club fights | Keep a defensive item ready and take a fast, controlled putt. | Play an aggressive direct shot only if the green is clear. |

<Callout type="warning" title="Hazard Alert">
Falling out of bounds in the City biome resets you to your last activated checkpoint with a stroke penalty. Skipping checkpoints on these vertical maps is highly risky and should only be attempted if you possess a mobility tool.
</Callout>

When playing on sloped city streets, remember that gravity will cause your ball to roll significantly further than on standard grass fairways. Reduce your swing power by roughly twenty percent when shooting downhill to prevent overshooting the landing zone.
