<Section icon={Users} title="Mastering the New Team Mode">

The **super battle golf attack on city update** introduces the official Team Mode, allowing players to divide into Red and Blue teams. This mode shifts the focus from individual speed to collective scoring and tactical interference.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Lobby Configuration">
    The host selects "Team Mode" in the private or public lobby settings. Players are then assigned to the Red or Blue team, with a maximum of 4 players per side.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Role Distribution">
    Successful teams designate "Runners" who focus on reaching the hole quickly and "Disruptors" who stay back to guard checkpoints or greens with offensive items.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Coordinated Attacks">
    Teams should synchronize item usage. For example, one player uses a **Smoke Bomb** to cover the green while another uses a **Railgun** to pick off opponents trying to enter the smoke.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Scoring Mechanics">
    The team score is calculated based on the finishing positions of all members. Helping a teammate finish is often more valuable than finishing first yourself.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="Team Synergy">
In Team Mode, you cannot be knocked out by your own teammates' club swings, allowing for aggressive "bodyguarding" strategies near the final cup.
</Callout>

The addition of Team Mode has revitalized the competitive scene, with Discord communities already organizing 4v4 tournaments focused on the new City maps. The strategy involves using the urban terrain to create "kill zones" where the opposing team cannot progress without being hit by a coordinated barrage of items.

</Section>
