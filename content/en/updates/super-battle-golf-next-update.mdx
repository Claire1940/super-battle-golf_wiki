<Section icon={Map} title="New Mechanics: Wind and Cracked Ice Systems">

Two major environmental mechanics define the winter update: dynamic wind currents and breakable ice sheets. These features introduce a high level of unpredictability to every shot, transforming previously simple fairways into high-risk obstacle courses.

The wind system is indicated by an arrow and speed meter at the top of the screen. Wind strength can range from a gentle breeze to a severe gale, directly pushing your golf ball mid-flight. When launching high-altitude shots, the wind can carry your ball far out of bounds or halt its forward momentum entirely. Players must learn to adjust their power levels and aim slightly off-target to compensate for the drift.

Additionally, the frozen lakes scattered across the new maps are no longer safe pathways. The ice sheets are fragile and can crack under pressure. Firing the Gun item directly at the ice or detonating explosives nearby will shatter the surface, revealing freezing water hazards below. This allows defensive players to set traps for anyone attempting to take shortcuts across the ice.
