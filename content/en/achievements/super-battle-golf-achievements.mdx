<Section icon={Target} title="The Cool Headed Challenge">

The "Cool Headed" achievement is widely considered the most difficult non-grind trophy in the game. It requires you to finish a full nine-hole round with a total stroke count at or below par. In a standard match, this is nearly impossible due to constant interference from other players.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Form a Pacifist Group">
    Gather four friends and agree to a "Pacifist Run." This means no rockets, no landmines, and no intentional collisions for the duration of the 9-hole course.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Select the Forest Course">
    The Forest maps generally offer the most predictable terrain. Avoid the Desert or Coastal maps where high winds and water hazards can easily add penalty strokes.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Focus on Approach Shots">
    Since you aren't fighting, take your time. Use the power slider carefully. Landing on the green in two strokes on a Par 4 is essential to give yourself a "Birdie" buffer.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Master the Putting Green">
    Avoid over-powering your putts. A missed short putt is the fastest way to fail this achievement. Aim for the "Gimme" range to ensure easy pars.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Achievement Unlocked">
Successfully completing a pacifist run not only nets you "Cool Headed" but often triggers the "Pacifist" achievement simultaneously if you finish a hole without harming a single soul.
</Callout>

</Section>

The Gauntlet and Showdown maps in the Desert course are designed with a funnel-like structure. If you set the item drop rate to "Low" or "None," you can focus entirely on the power and angle. Aim for roughly 95% power with a slight downward angle to account for the initial drop.
